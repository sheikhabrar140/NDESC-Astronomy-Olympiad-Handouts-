# NDESC-Astronomy-Olympiad-Handouts-

## Introduction

While preparing for International Astronomy and Astrophysics Olympiad(IOAA), I identified a significant gap in accessible resources, especially those aligned with the high school curriculum. Moreover, there were little to no structured materials available in the Bangla language, despite the majority of students in Bangladesh studying in Bangla-medium institutions.  

To address this gap, we at Notre Dame Eco and Space Club began developing our own comprehensive notes and carefully refined them to ensure clarity, and accessibility. This repository contains all published astronomy olympiad handouts, available in both Bangla and English, designed to support students preparing for national and international astronomy olympiads.

## Who are we ?

NDESC Interstellar is the academic team of the Notre Dame Eco and Space Club (NDESC). We are committed to promoting higher education in astronomy and astrophysics by providing free, high-quality learning resources.
